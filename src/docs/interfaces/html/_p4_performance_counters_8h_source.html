30  * Known quirks:
31  - OVF_PMI+FORCE_OVF counters must have an ireset value of -1.
32  This allows the regular overflow check to also handle FORCE_OVF
33  counters. Not having this restriction would lead to MAJOR
34  complications in the driver's "detect overflow counters" code.
35  There is no loss of functionality since the ireset value doesn't
36  affect the counter's PMI rate for FORCE_OVF counters.
37 
38  - In experiments with FORCE_OVF counters, and regular OVF_PMI
39  counters with small ireset values between -8 and -1, it appears
40  that the faulting instruction is subjected to a new PMI before
41  it can complete, ad infinitum. This occurs even though the driver
42  clears the CCCR (and in testing also the ESCR) and invokes a
43  user-space signal handler before restoring the CCCR and resuming
44  the instruction.
45 */
